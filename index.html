About

Effervescence is the Cultural Festival of IIIT Allahabad (A Centre of Excellence in Information Technology established by Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India). At IIITA, we believe that while academic merit is of paramount importance, cultural and sports skills, when incorporated into it, promote the integrated development of a student at an educational institution. Effervescence, thus, is an aspiring stride towards this developmental goal that we hope to achieve. The fest has grown spectacularly over the years, with a footfall of about 21,000 and 120+ colleges. Our online events are prominent, attracting participants from as many as 17 countries.
